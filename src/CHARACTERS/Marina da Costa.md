## THE MARTYR WHO WILL BEAR THE CROSS INTO THE EDGE OF THE WORLD

Marina is a Teresalian dark haired woman, about 170 cm tall, she is often seen with a pendant of some sort.

On the 12th of June, Marina woke up with sunlight at her desk. She woke up and existed for the first time at the age of 37. Without any recollection of her last years of life she set out to investigate herself. But first why is she hearing something or a group of someones dictating her actions?
