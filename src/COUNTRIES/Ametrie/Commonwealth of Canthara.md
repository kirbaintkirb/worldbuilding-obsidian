The Commonwealth of  Canthara or simply Canthara is a country in West [[Ametrie]], it is an Sanatorian Republic, following the liturgical text of [[Sanatorism]] as law. Canthara is located in the Bay of Lime, in the Archipelago of Canthohara in the mouth of the bay.
