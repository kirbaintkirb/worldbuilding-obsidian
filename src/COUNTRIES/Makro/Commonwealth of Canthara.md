The Commonwealth of Canthara or simply Canthara is a country in West [[Makro]], it is an Sanatorian Republic, following the liturgical text of [[Sanatorism]] as law. Canthara borders [[Telemach]], {Neighbors}. Canthara used to be a part of Telemach under [[Limme]] colonization, but achieved sovereignty with the Common Annulment of 2899, which gave Canthara independence.
