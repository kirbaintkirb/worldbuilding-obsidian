Globule (diminutive of globe) are continental structures completely surrounded and sunk by [[Catastrophe Zones]], containing an atmosphere, hydrosphere and lithosphere. Globules as we know them used to be part of a single unified spherical shape given their curvature and satellite analysis. That shape however is long gone, as the [[Stentorian Ruin]] completely altered the form of the planet.

Globules come in many shapes: true globules also known as elliptical globules, irregular globules and the theoretical spiral globules.

Globules are assumed to vaguely follow a certain curvature, which is the hypothetical curvature of the Original Object, however  due to the Stentorian, they have different heights in relation to each other, globules like [[Poles]], and [[Icro]], which are below and above other plates exist. Due to this the Core measurement schema was created, as a navigational tool that can consider all necessary axes, Latitude, Longitude and Core Altitude
