The world has gone though multiple eras, which are defined through cultural revolutions in the world, either human or natural. Because of this, [[Ametrie|Ametrian]] historians utilize the following order for eras:

- Pre-Stentorian (past  -  1 BS)
- Fractured ( 0 - 999 AS)
- Standstill (1000 - 1999)
- Reconstruction (1000 - 1999)
- Navigations (2000 - 2499)
- Current times (2500 - 2999)

The modern day is the year 999, AR (After Reconstruction) and year 2999 AS (After Stentorian)
