Manism, also known as Bijiism or the Biji School of Thought is a religion of Fortunaic religion, with origins in the greater Zhu empire, now modern [[Democratic Oumun]] and [[Hphagpa Dominion]]. Manism had a focus on the process of manufacturing, especially artisanship as a divine force, focused on the symbol of the human hand as the primordial tool, which wields all others.

Manism has origins in the early [[Reconstruction Era]], as the rebuilding of society was seen through the divine  hands of a talented few, who transferred knowledge orally.
