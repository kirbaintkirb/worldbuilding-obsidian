## The name of the world is Mirus

This document contains important details about Mirus. Writing for Mirus has a few rules:

## Macro scale:

- The main conflict is one of the spiritual and the material
	- Despite showing greater solutions, the spiritual does not nullify the scale of the material
	- Despite showcasing the importance of being grounded and aware of the physical phenomenons, much of the world shines through a holy lens.
	- The material affects the spiritual, the spiritual affects the material.
		- However that won't mean that both are correct or neither are correct, in a spiritual analysis, the spirit is correct, in a historical analysis the material is correct.
		- History is material, god has no place among men, she is transcendent
		- Religion is spiritual, iconoclasm does not break faith. it does break windows.

* Hopelessness rules inside of alienation, that alienation separates everyone from everything creating dissociation.
	* The beginning of alienation started the [[Stentorian Ruin]]
	* This dissociation manifests as the literal division of the plates, which warps any sense (Aeden was round, Mirus is)

* The common form of the state is one where religion and law are **NOT** separate. There is no distinction between church and state in most cases.
	* This does not mean however that you can't practice religion outside of the state process or that you can't participate in the state process without religion (see [[Rotatorism]])

* This is an industrialized world, [[City-State of Villuvi]] was the one who began the process.

* The preferred time of writing is the "Cold War" or the [[Clean Wars]], where Still-leaning [[Telemach]] and Rotation-leaning [[Krahvkond]] try to achieve majoritary influence across the globe.
	* The Clean Wars started at the end of the [[Yearly War]] where Villuvi got bombarded by an unknown event (a la Tunguska Event)
## Micro scale:

* Plates are the "continental" divisions, there are many inhabited and triple undiscovered.
	* Seven of them are inhabited and have travel:
		* Rosalia
		* Apihia
		* Makro
		* Ametrie
		* Fortuna
		* Poles
		* Kasha
* Between each plate are the [[Catastrophe Zones]] also known as Black Seas
	* They are the division of the plates and contain a mysterious substance that has kept the world from being completely destroyed
