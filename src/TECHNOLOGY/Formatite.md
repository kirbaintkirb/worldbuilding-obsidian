Formatite or simply Form is an odorless, achromatic vitreous material that is found on [[Catastrophe Zones]] and rarely near the North of [[Mirus]]. Formatite is used in [[Interplate Vessels]] as an addition tot he usual energy source.

Formatite as an element is poorly understood, though there have been empirical observations regarding it's properties, it features an unknown melting point, though it is malleable and ductile, which makes it similar to a metal, but it is a {{reverse Newtonian fluid}} which means at rest it has metallic properties but becomes a liquid when force is applied. It is the most common substance in the world, filling all the  gaps between [[Plates]] and therefore forming the Catastrophe Zones.

It's actual atomic components however feature no similarities to any existing elements, it is the zero-th element, featuring no protons, but rather imaginary particles that act as positive charges.

Form is a non-newtonian fluid, if enough pressure is applied to it it liquefies immediately, but when that stress ceases, it returns to being a glass-like amorphous solid.
